ABOUT FRUITOPIA

  • MISSION & VISION

    Fruitopia Food Corp. is dedicated to crafting delicious and nutritious Hopia by blending traditional flavors with natural fruit purees. We strive to provide the best customer experience while supporting local fruit farmers and vendors. Our vision is to become a leading brand in the food industry, known for innovation, quality, and sustainability, as we expand and grow in the coming years.

  • CORE VALUES

    At Fruitopia, we uphold continuous improvement, always striving to enhance our products and services. We operate with integrity, ensuring honesty and strong moral values in everything we do. Our commitment to innovation drives us to create unique flavors and textures in the food industry. With passion, we serve our customers wholeheartedly, delivering only the best quality snacks. Above all, we value teamwork, fostering a collaborative and harmonious environment to achieve our goals together.

ABOUT FRUITOPIA

Fruitopia Food Corporation is a student-founded partnership that innovates Hopia by incorporating local fruit fillings. The company supports local farmers and aims to become the leading healthy dessert brand with a delicious, sustainable, and nutritious product.

ANONG FILLING MO TODAY?

  • FILLING SILLY?

    A burst of playful sweetness, like a surprise twist on the classic hopia. The pastry is light, airy, and fun, with a hint of sweetness that makes your taste buds giggle. The filling, a balance of creamy and slightly tangy, gives off a whimsical vibe that feels like you're indulging in a sweet little secret.

  • FILLING LOVELY?

    Delicate and charming, this hopia flavor captures the warmth of a gentle hug. The sweetness is soft and soothing, with a hint of floral or fruity notes that dance lightly on your tongue. Each bite feels like a loving embrace, calming and graceful, perfect for those moments when you just want to feel adored.

  • FILLING BLISSFUL?

    A serene, heavenly bite that makes you feel like you’re floating in a cloud. The hopia’s rich, smooth filling and melt-in-your-mouth pastry create a perfect harmony, with a touch of sweetness that lingers in a peaceful, contented way. Every bite is like a moment of pure joy and relaxation, leaving you completely at ease.

  • FILLING CRAZY?

    Bold and unexpected, this hopia flavor is an explosion of contrasting tastes. The filling might be a twist of something spicy, nutty, or sour, creating a delightful contrast to the flaky, buttery exterior. It’s the kind of flavor that takes you by surprise and leaves you craving more—unpredictable, adventurous, and totally fun.
