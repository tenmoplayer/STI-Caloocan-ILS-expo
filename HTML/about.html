MISSION

Our company is committed to serve tasty and nourishing snack that will satisfy our customers. Fruitopia, transforms the traditional Hopia by adding the natural goodness of fruit purees, making it a lovely dessert as well as a filling snack which gives a fresh twist to this delicacy. This distinctive feature makes our product stand out to the market as it doesn't only contribute to the flavor but also enhance the nutritional profile.

VISION

Fruitopia company aims to produce the best and healthy Hopia in the food industry market. Offering the best customer service. To help Filipino fruit vendors and to support local fruit farmers. Our company is aiming to expand and to grow as a successful company in the next few years.
